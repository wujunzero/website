Research Topics

  • Vertex Cover
  • Dominating Set
  • Diversified Top-k Clique

I have broad interest in artificial intelligence and its applications. My research is concerned with the practical resolution of NP-hard problems, including Boolean Satisfiability problem, Minimum Vertex Cover Problem, Maximum Satisfiability Problem, Maximum (Weight) Clique Problem, Graph Coloring Problem. I am particularly interested in the combinatorial problem with the heuristic algorithm and exact algorithm.
